Abdukodir Khusanov — Short Bio

Centre-back | Manchester City | Uzbekistan

Abdukodir Khusanov (born 2004) is an Uzbek defender who moved to Manchester City after impressive performances in Europe. He previously played for Lens and has represented Uzbekistan at youth and senior levels. Known for his pace, strength and defensive awareness, Khusanov became the first Uzbek to sign for a Premier League club. (Sources below.)
